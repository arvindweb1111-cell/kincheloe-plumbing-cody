About Us

69 YEARS. ONE NAME. KINCHELOE.

Kincheloe Plumbing has been Cody's most trusted plumber since 1957. That's nearly seven decades of showing up on time, doing the job right, and treating every customer like a neighbour — because in a town like Cody, they are.
